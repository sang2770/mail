❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long are emails stored?
All emails are automatically deleted after 7 days to protect privacy. This is a temporary email service, so please don't use it for important communications.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes! You can add your own domain by configuring MX records to point to our mail server. Check our Custom Domain Setup page for detailed instructions.
Is this service free?
Yes, our basic temporary email service is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future for advanced users.
How do I report abuse or spam?
Please email us at abuse@temp-mail-free.com with details about the issue. Include any relevant email headers or screenshots.
Can I integrate this with my application?
Yes! We provide a RESTful API for developers. Check our API Documentation for complete integration details.
What if my domain verification fails?
Domain verification can take up to 48 hours due to DNS propagation. Make sure your MX record points to "mail.temp-mail-free.com" with priority 10. If it still doesn't work, contact us for help.
